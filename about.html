Mercator Mountain Association - from Ljubljana, Slovenia

This week we are leaving behind we had a busy schedule with activities at several locations. A group of members and leadership of Shoqata Bjeshkatare Tetova welcomed and led the Mercator Mountain Association - from Ljubljana, Slovenia with great professionalism. This activity besides being very successful, it also had a great echo. The large group of Slovenian guests led by our association also drew the attention of Albanian Prime Minister Edi Rama. The second group joins the US "Sharri" event from Prizren by climbing the Mount of Corfu from the Macedonia side to join the hosts at the summit.

CNVP Sustainable Use of Nature

As part of the visit organized by the office of CNVP Sustainable Use of Nature in Northern Macedonia, Shoqata Bjeshkatare Tetova from Tetovo had the honor of being a host of the Board of CNVP Foundation from the Netherlands, consisting of Mr. Maarten Bremer, z. Thijs Van Buren and Mrs Marianne Mayboom, accompanied by Mr. Nehat Ramadani CNVP Director - in Northern Macedonia, Mrs. Anila Karanxha Aliaj, CNVP Director for Albania and Mr. Xhevat Lushi, CNVP director for Kosovo. We are grateful that despite the busy schedule, friends from the Netherlands managed to be our guests for a few hours and together we walked the last segment of the trail marked by the CNVP grant to promote mountaineering, as a recreational-sport activity for all ages and that from the stations of Shipkovica to Maja e Jellak! The main part of the trip from Hill of the Sun to the location cleared of fallen timber was realized with 4x4 cars of US members "TETOVA".
