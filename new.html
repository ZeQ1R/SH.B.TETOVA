Our Connections

CNVP Sustainable Use of Nature in Northern Macedonia

As part of the visit organized by the office of CNVP Sustainable Use of Nature in Northern Macedonia, Shoqata Bjeshkatare Tetova from Tetovo had the honor of being a host of the Board of CNVP Foundation from the Netherlands, consisting of Mr. Maarten Bremer, z. Thijs Van Buren and Mrs Marianne Mayboom, accompanied by Mr. Nehat Ramadani CNVP Director - in Northern Macedonia, Mrs. Anila Karanxha Aliaj, CNVP Director for Albania and Mr. Xhevat Lushi, CNVP director for Kosovo. We are grateful that despite the busy schedule, friends from the Netherlands managed to be our guests for a few hours and together we walked the last segment of the trail marked by the CNVP grant to promote mountaineering, as a recreational-sport activity for all ages and that from the stations of Shipkovica to Maja e Jellak! ... The main part of the trip from Hill of the Sun to the location cleared of fallen timber was realized with 4x4 cars of US members "TETOVA". All the way through. the project coordinator Misa Hasipi informed the guests about the success of the project as well as the achievements and the impact that this project continues to have in the highlands of Sharr, mainly in the aspect of preserving biodiversity, promoting alternative tourism, as an economic potential for the residents of the highland villages. On our return from the summit of Jellak, we arranged a symbolic picnic with locally supplied products as a token of gratitude for the guests, and once again emphasized the importance of financial support of CNVP. We explained to the guests that picnic products are mainly purchased by local producers, in order to promote economic development of the family budget, which is in fact also one of the objectives of the CNVP grant implemented by the project "Sharri Cultural Heritage" implemented by the US “Tattoo”. After finishing with the picnic and just before nightfall, with 4x4 cars we returned our guests safely to the hotel, on Hill of the Sun.


Mercator Mountain Association - from Ljubljana, Slovenia

This week we are leaving behind we had a busy schedule with activities at several locations. A group of members and leadership of Shoqata Bjeshkatare Tetova welcomed and led the Mercator Mountain Association - from Ljubljana, Slovenia with great professionalism. This activity besides being very successful, it also had a great echo. The large group of Slovenian guests led by our association also drew the attention of Albanian Prime Minister Edi Rama. The second group joins the US "Sharri" event from Prizren by climbing the Mount of Corfu from the Macedonia side to join the hosts at the summit. The guests also shared their gratitude to our association, where we take the opportunity to thank them publicly. ... The third group consisting of about ten members participated in the celebration of the 6th anniversary of the founding of the Mountain Association "The Beauty of Evil" - Bogovina. The walk took place from the New Village to Lake Bogovina. We thank all the aforementioned hosts and guests for their continuous cooperation and interaction with our association. As we invite and encourage our current members and new members to join us for our upcoming activities. We feel that in this way we can contribute a little to raising awareness about caring for nature and our mental and physical health.
